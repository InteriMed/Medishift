# Welcoming Pages Functionalities Summary

## OVERVIEW
This document outlines the key functionalities that should be presented on the welcoming pages (Homepage, Professionals Page, Facilities Page) to attract and convert users to the MediShift platform.

---

## CORE VALUE PROPOSITION

### Platform Purpose
- **Healthcare Staffing Marketplace**: Digital platform connecting Swiss healthcare facilities with qualified healthcare professionals
- **Dual-Sided Platform**: Serves both facilities (employers) and professionals (job seekers)
- **Swiss-Focused**: Initially targeting French-speaking Switzerland healthcare facilities
- **Free Platform**: No registration fees, no hidden costs, no commitment required

---

## KEY FUNCTIONALITIES TO HIGHLIGHT

### 1. MARKETPLACE & MATCHING SYSTEM

#### For Healthcare Facilities:
- **Post Job Openings**: Quick and easy job posting system
- **Browse Qualified Professionals**: Access to verified healthcare professionals
- **Search & Filter**: Find professionals based on qualifications, availability, location
- **Direct Matching**: Automated matching system connecting facilities with suitable professionals
- **Real-Time Availability**: See professionals' availability in real-time

#### For Healthcare Professionals:
- **Browse Job Opportunities**: Access to available positions across healthcare facilities
- **Post Availability**: Set and manage your availability calendar
- **Smart Matching**: Get matched with relevant job opportunities
- **Flexible Work**: Choose assignments that fit your schedule
- **Location-Based Search**: Find opportunities near you

---

### 2. PROFILE MANAGEMENT & VERIFICATION

#### For Both User Types:
- **Secure Profile Creation**: Multi-step onboarding process
- **Verification System**: Verified professionals and facilities only
- **Professional Credentials**: Display qualifications, certifications, experience
- **Facility Information**: Company details, location, specialties
- **Profile Customization**: Complete control over profile visibility and information

#### Security Features:
- **Swiss Compliance**: nLPD (Swiss data protection law) ready
- **SSL Encryption**: Secure data transmission
- **Swiss Hosting**: Data hosted in Switzerland
- **GDPR Compliant**: European data protection standards

---

### 3. CALENDAR & AVAILABILITY MANAGEMENT

#### For Healthcare Professionals:
- **Interactive Calendar**: Set and manage your availability
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Control when you're available to work
- **Recurring Availability**: Set patterns for regular availability
- **Time Block Management**: Define specific time slots for work

#### For Healthcare Facilities:
- **Need Planning**: Post staffing needs with specific dates and times
- **Shift Management**: Manage multiple shifts and positions
- **Availability View**: See when professionals are available
- **Calendar Integration**: View all needs and matches in one place

---

### 4. CONTRACT & PAYROLL MANAGEMENT

#### Key Features:
- **Digital Contracts**: Automated contract generation and management
- **Electronic Signatures**: Sign contracts directly on the platform
- **Contract Tracking**: Monitor contract status (draft, pending, active, completed)
- **Payroll Handling**: Automated payroll processing
- **Document Management**: Secure storage of all contract documents
- **Compliance**: Swiss labor law compliant contracts

#### Benefits:
- **Simplified Administration**: Reduces HR paperwork burden
- **Time Savings**: Focus on patient care, not administrative tasks
- **Transparency**: Clear contract terms and payment processing
- **Automated Workflows**: Streamlined approval and activation process

---

### 5. DIRECT MESSAGING & COMMUNICATION

#### Features:
- **Real-Time Messaging**: Direct communication between facilities and professionals
- **Message Threads**: Organized conversation history
- **Notifications**: Stay updated on new messages
- **Secure Communication**: Encrypted messaging system
- **Mobile Access**: Message anytime, anywhere

#### Use Cases:
- **Discuss Position Details**: Clarify job requirements and expectations
- **Negotiate Terms**: Discuss contract terms before signing
- **Coordinate Logistics**: Plan shift details and logistics
- **Build Relationships**: Maintain ongoing communication

---

### 6. TEAM WORKSPACE (For Facilities)

#### Internal HR Management:
- **Staff Management**: Manage internal team members
- **Internal Scheduling**: Create and manage internal staff schedules
- **Time-Off Requests**: Handle vacation and leave requests
- **Team Member Profiles**: Manage employee information
- **Subletting**: Share team members with other facilities when needed

#### Operational Settings:
- **Standard Opening Hours**: Define facility operating hours
- **Minimum Staffing Levels**: Set required staffing for different roles/shifts
- **Approval Workflows**: Customize time-off approval processes
- **Role Management**: Assign different permission levels to team members

---

### 7. SEARCH & DISCOVERY FEATURES

#### Advanced Search:
- **Filter by Location**: Find opportunities/professionals by geographic area
- **Filter by Specialty**: Search by healthcare specialty or role
- **Filter by Availability**: Find matches based on time requirements
- **Filter by Qualifications**: Search by certifications and credentials
- **Filter by Experience**: Find professionals with specific experience levels

#### Discovery Tools:
- **Recommended Matches**: AI-powered suggestions based on preferences
- **Trending Opportunities**: See popular or urgent positions
- **Saved Searches**: Save search criteria for quick access
- **Alerts**: Get notified when new matching opportunities appear

## SUMMARY

The welcoming pages should emphasize:
1. **Simplicity**: Easy to use, quick setup
2. **Freedom**: Free platform, no commitment
3. **Efficiency**: Automated processes, time-saving
4. **Security**: Swiss compliance, data protection
5. **Flexibility**: Control over schedule and opportunities
6. **Quality**: Verified professionals and facilities
7. **Support**: Direct communication and assistance

The goal is to clearly communicate that MediShift solves real problems for both healthcare facilities (staffing shortages, administrative burden) and professionals (flexible work opportunities, simplified processes) while maintaining the highest standards of security and compliance.
