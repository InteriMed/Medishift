4. Data Sharing

4.1 PayrollPlus (Staff Leasing Partner)

When a shift is confirmed, we share necessary data with our staff leasing partner PayrollPlus AG for:

  • Salary payments to healthcare professionals
  • Social insurance contributions
  • Tax withholding compliance
  • Commission processing

PayrollPlus acts as the Employer of Record and processes data under Swiss employment law.

4.2 Swiss Health Registry (BAG)

We verify GLN numbers against the official Swiss Federal Office of Public Health registry. This is a legal requirement for healthcare staffing.
